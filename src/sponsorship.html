Call For Sponsorship

ICVGIP 2024 marks the 15th edition of the Indian Conference on Computer Vision, Graphics, and Image Processing. As India's premier conference, ICVGIP has been a vital platform for presenting technological advancements and research findings since its inception in 1998. ICVGIP 2024 is being organized by IIIT-Bangalore in association with the Indian Unit for Pattern Recognition and Artificial Intelligence (IUPRAI), an affiliate of the International Association for Pattern Recognition (IAPR), the conference is renowned for fostering a strong community of researchers and enthusiasts from India and abroad. Transitioning to an annual event from 2022, due to the substantial growth in the community, ICVGIP 2024 continues to embody our dedication to the fields of computer vision, graphics, and image processing. ICVGIP offers a vibrant environment for academic and industry professionals to engage, exchange ideas, and explore the latest innovations within these domains.

ICVGIP has consistently enjoyed robust industry relationships and encourages participation from various sectors. The conference invites industry involvement at multiple levels, including the technical program, research symposiums, and exhibitions. Engaging with ICVGIP as a sponsor not only allows for significant visibility but also the opportunity to shape the discourse in these rapidly evolving fields. We strongly encourage companies and R&D laboratories to present their work and engage actively with the community at ICVGIP. This engagement is not only beneficial for networking and knowledge exchange but also instrumental in shaping future directions in computer vision, graphics, and image processing.
