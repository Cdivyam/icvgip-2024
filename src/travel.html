Travel Guidelines

We will update this page for travel guidelines to reach the venue from the Kempegowda International Airport (BLR-Airport) and KSR Railway Station (Majestic Station/City Railway Station/KSR Bengaluru Railway Station).
The following guide should be useful for you to plan your travel with comfort and economy.

From Airport

IIIT-Bangalore is 40Km away from the Bengaluru airport.

Cabs
  • Availability: All the major cab aggregators work in Bengaluru - Ola, Uber, Rapido, Meru, BluSmart. We suggest you to use these apps, know the price yourself and book in advance.
  • Cost: Cabs are more expensive and it can cost upwards of 2k to and from airport depending on availablity, time of travel, etc.
  • Time:
    • It takes around 1.5 - 3 hours to travel to IIIT-Bangalore from airport via cab depending on the time of day.
Bus
  • Availability: Bangalore has a strong network of Airport Buses with good AC Facility.
    • The Bus: KIA-8 starts from Airport and will drop you 1 km away from IIIT-Bangalore. The Bus is air conditioned with high availability, reliability and also trackable on "Namma BMTC" app. We strongly suggest you to use these for travel to and from airport.
  • Cost: 350Rs
  • Time: It takes around 2 - 3 hours to travel to IIIT-Bangalore from airport via KIA-8 depending on the time of day.

From Railway Station

IIIT-Bangalore is 25Km away from the KSR Railway Station.
The best modes of transport will be cabs/auto-rickshaws from here.

Cabs/Auto-rickshaw
  • Availability: All the major cab aggregators work in Bengaluru - Ola, Uber, Rapido, Meru, BluSmart. We suggest you to use these apps, know the price yourself and book in advance.
  • For Autos: While autos are available on Ola, Uber and Rapido, there is local auto aggregator app which you may checkout - "Namma Yatri".
  • Cost: Cabs are more expensive and it can cost upwards of 500Rs to and from KSR depending on availablity/ time of travel, etc.
  • Time:
    • It takes around 1.5 - 2 hours to travel to IIIT-Bangalore from airport via cab depending on the time of day.
