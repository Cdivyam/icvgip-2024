Call for Papers: The Young Faculty Symposium

The prestigious ICVGIP 2024 invites young faculty to showcase their ambitious, ground-breaking ideas!! This is a great opportunity to reach out to willing audience - students, industry and academic senior researchers, gain invaluable feedback and explore beyond.

Why Submit?
  • Exclusive Platform: Showcase your exploration and share your insights, challenges, and breakthroughs in relevant domains.
  • Engagement: Participate in vibrant discussions with leading experts and peers.
Eligibility
  • Young faculty with a Ph.D. degree with an overall work experience of less than 8 years as a Principal Investigator working in engineering & science in an academic institution.
  • PIs working on problems related to computer vision, image processing, pattern recognition, and computer graphics can submit the abstracts.
  • Submissions to align with the broad topics under consideration by conference main track.
  • PIs whose work is published, under review, or ongoing/about to be submitted, are also encouraged to submit their abstracts to ICVGIP'24.
Submission Details

Submissions should be in the form of a 2-page extended abstract, formatted according to the [instructions] available on the conference website.

The 2-page extended abstract must be submitted into the CMT3 submission portal as a single PDF file.

Review and Presentation

All submissions will be peer-reviewed by a committee of experts from both academia and industry to ensure high quality and relevance. Authors of accepted papers will have the opportunity to present their work as a poster and/or a brief presentation at the conference. Kindly note, the symposium papers will not be published as part of the ACM proceedings.

We eagerly await your contributions and look forward to seeing you at the Young Faculty Symposium at ICVGIP 2024.

Important Dates

Paper Submission Deadline: 1 September, 2024

Extended Paper Submission Deadline: 20 September, 2024

Acceptance Notification: 10 October, 2024

Camera-ready version due: 15 October, 2024 22 November, 2024
